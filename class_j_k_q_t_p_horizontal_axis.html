Detailed Description

implements a horizontal axis, based on JKQTPCoordinateAxis (for most of documentation: see JKQTPCoordinateAxis).

The positioning of the different axis elements depends on the psition of the "other" axis (here y-axis), i.e. the corresponding vertical axis:
